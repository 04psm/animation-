iPhone 8 Plus


The iPhone 8 has been launched alongside the more expensive iPhone X. It looks much like its predecessor, the iPhone 7, but introduces a new glass back to allow for wireless charging. It's available in Space Grey, Silver, and Gold, with 64GB and 256GB storage options at launch time. You get Apple's powerful A11 Bionic processor with six cores, which has beaten the current-generation processors used in flagship Android smartphones. The display now supports True Tone colour temperature adjustment to match ambient lighting.
